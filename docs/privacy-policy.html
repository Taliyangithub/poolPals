PoolPals – Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 30, 2026

PoolPals respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

a. Account Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • User ID

b. Ride Information

  • Ride details you create (locations, time, available seats)
  • Ride requests and approvals

c. Messages

Messages sent within the app are stored to enable communication and for safety, moderation, and abuse prevention purposes.

d. Technical Information

We may collect basic technical data such as timestamps and device-related information for security and operational purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to:

  • Provide core app functionality
  • Enable ride coordination between users
  • Facilitate messaging
  • Maintain platform safety and integrity

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we process your data based on:

  • Your consent
  • Performance of a contract (providing the app service)
  • Legitimate interests (security, fraud prevention, abuse reporting)

4. Data Sharing

PoolPals does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data may be processed by trusted infrastructure providers (such as cloud hosting and authentication services) strictly for app functionality.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide the service.

When you delete your account:

  • Your account data is deleted or anonymized
  • Your active ride participation is removed
  • Seat availability is updated accordingly

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability

You can exercise these rights directly within the app or by contacting us.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children’s Privacy

PoolPals is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed outside your country of residence. Where required, appropriate safeguards are applied in accordance with GDPR.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
